Skills and My Creative Process

I am an experienced software developer with experience in both front and back end website development, mobile app development, desktop app development, and many other languages and technologies that are applicable in many other areas of the software development space.

My creative process starts with understanding the customers vision for the project. We'll start by having a conversation about what you'd like to see, and I'll try to provide my professional input to help you come away with the best finished product possible. Then, I'll do some research and mock-ups on my own and present it all to you so you can see what I'd like to do before any actual development happens. Finally, the majority of my time is spent actually digging into the code and pumping out a great final product. Check out the next sections for some things I've done in the past. I hope you'll consider talking with me about your next big idea!
